Nick Welch, President

Nick Welch began his career in the title industry at age 19. Working in the Illinois Basin oil industry, Nick was mentored by oil industry legends C.E. Brehm and Marvin Craddock. To this day, he still applies the tools of the trade he learned in the early years of his career.


Throughout his 39 years in the title and acquisition business, Nick has worked in nearly every industry possible. His experience stretches across the coal, oil, gas, wireless telecom, fiber-optic, right-of-way, commercial real estate, and residential real estate industries.


Over the course of his career, Nick has closed over 85 million dollars of commercial transactions, acquired over 10,000 acres of minerals for coal, oil, and gas exploration; and performed title curative work on over 100 miles of fiber-optic right-of-way.


Nick has acquired hundreds of cell sites, over 100 miles of fiber-optic right-of-way, and posted a 99%+ success rating in more than 150 zoning hearings.


Nick’s experience spans 27 different states.


Family

Nick Welch lives in Greenwood, Indiana. He and his wife, Anne, have 7 children and 9 grand-children. In his free time, Nick is an avid guitarist. He has a band with 3 of his sons, performing at festivals and events in the Indianapolis area. According to Nick, his greatest achievement is his faith and his family.
